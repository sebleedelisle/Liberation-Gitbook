# ✅ Pause or cancel payments

Recurring paid licenses auto-renew each month, but you can pause or cancel future payments from your account.

If you pause payments, your license stays active until the end of the current paid period. When the pause starts, paid access stops until the license restarts. If you cancel payments permanently, your license stays active until the end of the current paid period, then returns to Free mode and no further payments will be taken.

Log in to the website, open the [_Your licenses_](https://liberationlaser.com/account/my-products) page, then choose the license you want to manage.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/account-active-licence.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

## Pause payments

If payment pausing is available for your license, click _PAUSE OR CANCEL PAYMENTS_.

Under _Pause payments_, choose when payments should restart:

* _Next month_
* _On a date I choose_
* _Only when I restart manually_

Then click _PAUSE PAYMENTS_.

Your license page will show _Payments paused from_ and the paid period end date. Your license will stay active until that date.

Before the pause starts, you can click _REACTIVATE PAYMENTS_ to cancel the scheduled pause. Payments will then continue as normal.

After the pause starts, your license page will show _License paused_. From there you can restart the license now, change the restart date, pause until you restart manually, or cancel payments permanently.

When a paused license restarts, Paddle will take payment using your saved payment method.

## Cancel payments permanently

To stop future payments without scheduling a restart, use _Cancel payments permanently_ and click _CANCEL PAYMENTS_.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/account-cancel-auto-renew.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

After cancellation, the license page will show _Auto-renew is off_ and the date your paid license remains active until. To use paid features again after that, you'll need to start over with a new license.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/account-auto-renew-off.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
