# 🟩 Alt zone system

Have you ever been in a situation where you might need to instantly move or disable a selection of zones? For example :

* Lasers going in to the sky that you need to redirect to terminate onto buildings if an aircraft goes by.
* Lasers that enter an unused balcony level that you need to redirect in certain parts of the show while crew are there.

The Alt zone system lets you create an alternate zone for any existing zone, and with a single button, switch to all the alternates at once.

### Adding an alternate zone

In the output view, right-click on a zone and click _ADD ALT ZONE_. Notice that a new green colored zone appears. You can edit and adjust this in exactly the same way as any of your zones. You'll need to enable Alt zones in the top icon bar in order to see the test patterns.

### Switching to your Alt zones

As soon as you add any alt zone a toggle button appears in the top icon bar labeled _ALT ZONES._ Click this button to toggle between your alt zones and your regular zones.

### How to disable instead of move a zone

Add an Alt zone, right-click on it and click the _Disable output_ button (circle with a line through it). Now when you enable Alt zones, content will be redirected to this now disabled alt zone.
