# ✅ Katkıda Bulunanlar

Liberation, [Seb Lee-Delisle](https://app.gitbook.com/u/ylIaEEh1DCgBfWngNL7qhvoUTQV2) tarafından tasarlanmış ve programlanmıştır.

Windows sürümüne [Dan Moore](https://www.makeitdoathing.com) destek vermiştir.

Aşağıdaki açık kaynak projeleri kullanır:

#### [openFrameworks](https://openframeworks.cc)

openFrameworks katkıda bulunanlarının [tam listesi](https://github.com/openframeworks/openFrameworks/blob/master/THANKS.md)

openFrameworks, platformunuza bağlı olarak aşağıdaki [kütüphanelerin](https://github.com/openframeworks/openFrameworks/blob/master/docs/libraries.md) bir birleşimini kullanır:

[OpenGL](http://www.opengl.org/), [GLEW](http://glew.sourceforge.net/), [GLUT](http://www.opengl.org/resources/libraries/glut/), [libtess2](https://code.google.com/p/libtess2/), [cairo](http://cairographics.org/), [rtAudio](http://www.music.mcgill.ca/~gary/rtaudio/), [PortAudio](http://www.portaudio.com/), [FMOD](http://www.fmod.org/), [Kiss FFT](http://kissfft.sourceforge.net/), [FreeType](http://freetype.sourceforge.net/index2.html), [FreeImage](http://freeimage.sourceforge.net/), [Quicktime](https://developer.apple.com/standards/classic-quicktime/), [Unicap](http://unicap-imaging.org/), [GStreamer](http://gstreamer.freedesktop.org/), [videoInput](https://github.com/ofTheo/videoInput), [Poco](http://pocoproject.org/)

#### openFrameworks eklentileri

* [ofxAbletonLink](https://github.com/comoc/ofxAbletonLink), [Akihiro Komori](https://github.com/comoc) tarafından
* [ofxArtnet](https://github.com/hiroyuki/ofxArtnet), [Hiroyuki Hori](https://github.com/hiroyuki) tarafından
* [ofxLTC](https://github.com/2bbb/ofxLTC), [2bit](https://github.com/2bbb) tarafından ([libltc](https://github.com/x42/libltc) kullanır)
* [ofxMidi](https://github.com/danomatika/ofxMidi), [Dan Wilcox](https://github.com/danomatika) tarafından ([Gary Scavone](https://github.com/thestk/rtmidi) tarafından geliştirilen [RTMIDI](https://github.com/garyscavone) kullanır)
* [ofxNative](https://github.com/kritzikratzi/ofxNative), [Hansi Raber](https://github.com/kritzikratzi) tarafından
* [ofxSVG](https://github.com/openframeworks/openFrameworks/tree/master/addons/ofxSvg) ([libsvgtiny](https://source.netsurf-browser.org/libsvgtiny.git/) kullanır)

#### [Dear ImGui](https://github.com/ocornut/imgui)

UI kısmen, Omar Cornut tarafından geliştirilen ve GitHub’daki tüm doğrudan veya dolaylı [katkıda bulunanların](https://github.com/ocornut/imgui) desteğiyle oluşturulan [Dear ImGui](https://github.com/ocornut/imgui/graphs/contributors) kullanılarak hazırlanmıştır.
