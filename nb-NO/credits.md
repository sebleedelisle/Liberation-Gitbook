# ✅ Kreditering

Liberation er designet og programmert av [Seb Lee-Delisle](https://app.gitbook.com/u/ylIaEEh1DCgBfWngNL7qhvoUTQV2).

Hjelp med Windows-versjonen fra [Dan Moore](https://www.makeitdoathing.com).

Den bruker følgende prosjekter med åpen kildekode:

#### [openFrameworks](https://openframeworks.cc)

[Full liste](https://github.com/openframeworks/openFrameworks/blob/master/THANKS.md) over bidragsytere til openFrameworks

openFrameworks bruker en kombinasjon av følgende [biblioteker](https://github.com/openframeworks/openFrameworks/blob/master/docs/libraries.md), avhengig av plattformen din:

[OpenGL](http://www.opengl.org/), [GLEW](http://glew.sourceforge.net/), [GLUT](http://www.opengl.org/resources/libraries/glut/), [libtess2](https://code.google.com/p/libtess2/), [cairo](http://cairographics.org/), [rtAudio](http://www.music.mcgill.ca/~gary/rtaudio/), [PortAudio](http://www.portaudio.com/), [FMOD](http://www.fmod.org/), [Kiss FFT](http://kissfft.sourceforge.net/), [FreeType](http://freetype.sourceforge.net/index2.html), [FreeImage](http://freeimage.sourceforge.net/), [Quicktime](https://developer.apple.com/standards/classic-quicktime/), [Unicap](http://unicap-imaging.org/), [GStreamer](http://gstreamer.freedesktop.org/), [videoInput](https://github.com/ofTheo/videoInput), [Poco](http://pocoproject.org/)

#### openFrameworks addons

* [ofxAbletonLink](https://github.com/comoc/ofxAbletonLink) av [Akihiro Komori](https://github.com/comoc)
* [ofxArtnet](https://github.com/hiroyuki/ofxArtnet) av [Hiroyuki Hori](https://github.com/hiroyuki)
* [ofxLTC](https://github.com/2bbb/ofxLTC) av [2bit](https://github.com/2bbb) (bruker [libltc](https://github.com/x42/libltc))
* [ofxMidi](https://github.com/danomatika/ofxMidi) av [Dan Wilcox](https://github.com/danomatika) (bruker [RTMIDI](https://github.com/thestk/rtmidi) av [Gary Scavone](https://github.com/garyscavone))
* [ofxNative](https://github.com/kritzikratzi/ofxNative) av [Hansi Raber](https://github.com/kritzikratzi)
* [ofxSVG](https://github.com/openframeworks/openFrameworks/tree/master/addons/ofxSvg) (bruker [libsvgtiny](https://source.netsurf-browser.org/libsvgtiny.git/))

#### [Dear ImGui](https://github.com/ocornut/imgui)

UI-et er delvis bygget med [Dear ImGui](https://github.com/ocornut/imgui), utviklet av Omar Cornut og alle direkte og indirekte [bidragsytere](https://github.com/ocornut/imgui/graphs/contributors) på GitHub.
