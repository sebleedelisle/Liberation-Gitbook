# ✅ Cancel your licence

Your licence auto-renews each month, but you can turn this off at any time. You’ll keep access to paid features until the end of your current licence period. After that, your account will return to Free mode and you won’t be charged again.

Log in to the website, open the [_Your licences_](https://liberationlaser.com/account/my-products) page, choose the licence you want to manage, then click _CANCEL PAYMENTS_.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/account-active-licence.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

On the confirmation page, check the paid period end date and click _CANCEL PAYMENTS_ again.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/account-cancel-auto-renew.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

After cancellation, the licence page will show _Auto-renew is off_ and the date your paid licence remains active until.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/account-auto-renew-off.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
